Frequently asked questions

Who can use Mygenomanalytics.com?

Mygenomanalytics.com is for anyone who took their DNA test with company 23andme and wants to know more about their genes.

What if I haven’t received my raw data yet?

You are still welcome to browse through the website. The views have been prepopulated with mock data so that you already have a flavor of how it would look like. As soon as you upload your files, the content will be replaced with your own data.

How is this website different than other genomics companies?

Mygenomanalytics.com provides complementary analysis that includes an attempt to link which trait came from which ancestor and a deep dive into your rare mutations. Mygenomenalytics.com also includes traits at the forefront of research not covered by 23andme such as: facial morphology, intelligence, mathematical ability, autism, longevity and many others.

How long does it take for my results to load?

The file upload takes an average of 30 seconds and usually doesn’t exceed 1 minute. If your experience differs from these estimates, please kindly close and reopen your browser or clear cookies.

How much knowledge of biology do I need to understand my results?

There is no prerequisite, although it could be helpful to have some basic understanding of what an “SNP” is. Each Results Tab provides some help on how to interpret the results.

What is an SNP ?

SNP stands for “single nucleotid polymorphism”. You can see it as a difference in your DNA that makes you different from other people. Some SNPs may be associated to a known trait such as the color of your eyes. It can be of particular interest to look at which of your trait’s expression is not commonly found in the general population, which is what we are looking at here .

Where can I find my genome and ancestry files ?

The genome file, which contains your raw data is available for download in a txt file . The ancestry file, which contains your genomic coordinates is available for download in a CSV format.

How accurate is Mygenomanalytics.com?

You may have noticed that most views in Mygenomanalytics.com allow you to choose your desired p-value threshold. The lower the p-value , the greater the statistical significance of the observed result. A p-value of 0.05 or lower is generally considered statistically significant.

What is the purpose of the “Download” tab?

The download tab will provide you with a master table in csv format containing your raw data. This information may be more relevant for professionals or data scientists in the research field who are interested in performing their own analysis or use it as benchmark for their own models. The download feature is only activated if you have uploaded your own data.

How does Mygenomanalytics.com protect my privacy?

Your data is permanently deleted as soon as you close your browser or after 12 hours of inactivity. The only way to regenerate your results is to upload your files again.
